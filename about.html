MEAT WALLET


ABOUT

Formed in Peoria Arizona, the members of Meat Wallet, Jonathan Breasar,
John Thompson, and Kendall Callison, have been slappin skins and
strings for years. Their act is known as the best ever heard from within the
scene. "There really is no other Meat Wallet out there," one listener
once had said. The local underground band is perfect for any
occasion whether it's a birthday party, wedding, venue, or funeral.
Meat Wallet will have you on your toes.
